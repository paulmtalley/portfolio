Food Pantries in Albuquerque, New Mexico

This map shows five of the most popular food pantries in Albuquerque.

Food insecurity is one of the major economic problems affecting people in the United States and around the world. Food pantries offer vital relief when people are in need. Food pantries—which can be provided by churches, temples, non-profits, or governmental organizations—are a go-to resource for people who are homeless or who cannot afford to buy food.

The map on this page shows only five of many food pantries in Albuquerque. The five that are included here are:

  • Roadrunner Food Bank
  • Joy Junction
  • Barrett House
  • St. Martin's Hospitality Center
  • The Storehouse
