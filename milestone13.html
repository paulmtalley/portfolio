Fires in New Mexico, 1911 - 2014

This map shows fire perimeters in New Mexico from 1911 to 2014. The goal is to provide context between the locations of past fires in relation to towns and state parks within the state. Fire perimeters are shown in red outlines. State parks are the green polygons, towns (with population over 5000) are shown as blue polygons, and the county boundaries are shown in pink. Data was collected from RGIS.
